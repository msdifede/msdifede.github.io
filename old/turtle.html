2016-2017 Queensborough Internet and Information Technology Program

This was the first year of the partnership program between Queensborough Community College and Mineola High School. We learned a lot of things this year including:

  • How to build websites
  • How to build robots
  • How to install Windows
  • How to go to Washington DC
  • What a three period class feels like
  • Things aren't always what they seem, sometimes you have to look underneath
  • Computer Science is a high paying field
